Some common Questions
Were often asked.

+ My flowers are falling off or dying?

Possible causes for your flowers falling off or dying include inadequate watering, insufficient sunlight, or nutrient deficiencies. Ensure proper watering, provide adequate sunlight, and consider fertilizing to address potential nutrient issues

+ What causes leaves to become pate?

Leaf paleness can result from insufficient sunlight, nutrient deficiencies like iron or nitrogen, or overwatering that limits root oxygen uptake. Assess lighting conditions, adjust fertilization, and ensure proper watering to address leaf paleness.
